All proprietary algorithms, microservice structuring topologies, and front-end code (except open-source dependencies where noted) are entirely owned by Darion Technologies. 3. Service Level Agreements (SLA)

Core uptime structures guarantee an automated 99.9% reliability natively supported by our edge redundancy mechanisms. Unplanned outages resulting in prolonged operational downtime will trigger standard contractual compensation as previously negotiated by Darion legal divisions.

4. Limitation of Liability

Darion Technologies isolates network risk through Zero-Trust paradigms. Nonetheless, Darion remains legally absolved from incidental macro-fail damages triggered from external vulnerabilities unassociated with the immediate Darion codebase itself (e.g. foundational weaknesses in underlying standard node hardware).
