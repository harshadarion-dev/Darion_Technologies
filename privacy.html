Privacy Policy

At Darion Technologies, safeguarding your operational data is our foundational priority. Read our commitment to Zero-Trust information routing.

1. Information Collection 2. Usage and Logging 3. Third-party Pipelines 4. Your Data Rights (GDPR)

1. Information Collection

When engaging with Darion Technologies' software ecosystems, we collect only the essential metadata required to establish secure API handshakes. We operate on a logic of minimization: if the data is not strictly required to verify the authentication layer via JSON Web Tokens, it is never queried.

Our distributed architecture caches analytical traffic explicitly on an opt-in basis when configuring instances of the Darion Core Banking Platform or the User Logistics software.

2. Usage and Logging

We analyze macro-level trends across our Edge clusters to route traffic efficiently. Individual payload bodies are automatically encrypted upon submission using AES-256 standards, rendering the data entirely opaque to Darion engineers unless explicitly granted decryption keys during a targeted support escalation.

3. Third-party Pipelines

Darion Technologies fundamentally refuses to monetize operational telemetry. Your metadata is never sold to external vendors. When third-party interactions are strictly required (e.g., Stripe API for SaaS billing, AWS for container orchestration), data transmission occurs over strictly verified mutual TLS tunnels.

4. Your Data Rights (GDPR)

You possess absolute ownership of the datasets injected into your Darion-hosted software stack. Pursuant to standard GDPR compliance pipelines naturally structured into our software databases, you may export or trigger a total cascading deletion of your institutional records instantaneously through your assigned administrator dashboard.
